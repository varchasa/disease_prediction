About :

Leukemia is a cancer of the blood or bone marrow. Bone marrow produces blood cells. Leukemia can happen when there is a problem with the production of blood cells. It usually affects the leukocytes, or white blood cells.


Symptoms:

  • Tiredness
  • Weight loss
  • Having fevers or chills
  • Poor blood clotting
  • Frequent infections
  • Nausea
  • Night sweats
  • Flu-like symptoms
  • Bone pain


Preacautions:

Prevention of leukemia is not so easy because we still don't know it exact cause and some of its risk factors cannot be avoided, like age. Not easy doesn't mean impossible .The following is the ways that may prevent leukemia: Quit Smoking, Limit Benzene Exposure, and Reduce Radiation Exposure.
